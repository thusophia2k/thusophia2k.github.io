Air shower

X

Air showers are the specialised chambers which people must pass through before entering cleanrooms in order to decontaminate. Air showers are considered essential equipment in fields where sterile or dust-free conditions are critical. The biggest contaminant in cleanroom is the fine particles attached to clothing, footwear and materials. Air shower helps to remove such fine particles by using air jet to blow at cleanroom clothing and materials before entering a cleanroom. ORAI VN air showers are available in a variety of customized sizes and configurations to suit specifications of the clients.
